# About bot
Our bot is designed with user convenience at the forefront: anyone can test it and see how it works in practice. The intuitive interface and straightforward setup mean you can get started in just a few clicks — no technical expertise required. [[1]](#users-subscriptions)
The main idea behind *BoostBot* was conceived in 2025. It emerged from a simple observation: many users struggle with fragmented tools for everyday tasks. We wanted to create a unified, smart assistant that adapts to individual needs, automates routine actions, and saves valuable time. Since then, we’ve been refining the concept, incorporating user feedback and the latest advancements in technology to make the experience as seamless as possible.
### About privacy policy
You don’t have to worry about your privacy — we take data protection seriously. **BoostBot requests  only surface-level data, such as nicknames and your hobbies**...

Here’s what we **don’t** collect:
- sensitive personal details (full name, address, financial data);
- private messages or conversations;
- location data unless explicitly granted for a specific feature;
- any data from third‑party accounts beyond what’s necessary for authentication.

*For a comprehensive overview of our data practices, please refer to the **Privacy Policy** section in the official documentation.*

### User's subscriptions
**[1]** Our bot is completely FREE to use. This makes it accessible to everyone.
However, if you want to unlock *advanced capabilities*, test *premium features*, or exceed standard usage limits, we offer **flexible pricing plans** designed to suit different needs:
- **:white_check_mark: Basic (Free)** 
- **Pro**
- **Enterprise**
> You can upgrade or downgrade at any time, ensuring you only pay for what you actually need.
